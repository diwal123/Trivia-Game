4 Pic 1 Word


Rules

4 Pics 1 Word's gameplay is very simple: each question displays four pictures linked by one word - the player's aim is to guess out what the word is, from a set of words given below the pictures.

For each correct answer, a score of 1 will be added.

There are altogether 20 questions. For each question, 10 seconds will be allocated and next question will load in few seconds.
